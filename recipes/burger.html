Ranch Burger

Description

Lean grilled beef burgers are served on whole wheat buns with onion slices, lettuce, tomato, and creamy ranch dressing.

Great for any weeknight or weekend meal, sure to be a favourite for the whole family!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/4 cup soft bread crumbs
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 tsp seasoning salt
  • 1 medium red onion, sliced
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4 whole-wheat buns, toasted
  • Romaine lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • 1/4 cup creamy ranch dressing

Steps

  1. Combine Ground Beef, bread crumbs, egg white and seasoned salt in medium bowl, mixing lightly but thoroughly. Lightly shape into four 1/2-inch thick patties. Brush onion slices with 1/2 tablespoon oil.
  2. Place patties and onion slices on grid over medium, ash-covered coals. Grill patties, covered, 8 to 10 minutes (over medium heat on preheated gas grill 7 to 9 minutes), until instant-read thermometer inserted horizontally into center registers 160 degrees F, turning occasionally. Grill onions, 11 to 13 minutes or until tender, brushing with remaining 1/2 tablespoon oil and turning occasionally.
  3. Line bottom of each bun with lettuce. Place tomatoes and burgers on lettuce. Spoon dressing over burgers; top with onions. Close sandwiches.
